A _minimalist_ and _efficient_ website tailored for a business offering **gifts** and **promotional products**, featuring a _diverse catalog_ ranging from stationery, eco-friendly bags, to seasonal gifts. With a _clean_, **fast-loading**, and _user-friendly_ design, the site supports _brand customization_ like logo printing, ideal for _businesses_, **events**, and _promotional campaigns_.

### **Core Technologies**

- **Next.js**: _Modern frontend_ for _high speed_ and _SEO optimization_.
- **NestJS**: _Robust backend_ for _efficient_ business logic processing.
- **MySQL**: _Reliable database_ for managing _products_ and _orders_.
- **WordPress**: Integrated _CMS_ for _easy_ content management.
- **E-commerce**: Supports _online transactions_, _cart management_, and _secure payments_.

### **Key Features**

- **Diverse Catalog**: Offers _stationery_, **eco-friendly bags**, _seasonal gifts_, and various _practical promotional items_.
- **Flexible Customization**: Enables _logo printing_ and _product personalization_ for business needs.
- **Intuitive Design**: _Modern_ interface, easy to navigate, _optimized for user experience_.
- **Event Support**: Perfect for _exhibitions_, **open campus events**, _company anniversaries_, or _community initiatives_.

This website is a _comprehensive solution_ for businesses to _promote their brand_ through _high-quality_, _customized_ products, delivering a _seamless_ and _effective_ online shopping experience.
