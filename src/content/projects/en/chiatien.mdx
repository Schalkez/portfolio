A _practical_ and **user-friendly** web application designed to **simplify** expense splitting among friends or groups after _social gatherings_, **trips**, or **events**. Built on **React**, this tool enables _quick_ calculation and distribution of shared costs, ensuring **fairness** and _transparency_.

### **Technologies Used**

- **React**: Smooth frontend interface for an _enhanced user experience_.
- **Zustand**: _Lightweight_ and **efficient** state management.
- **Tailwind CSS**: _Modern_, **responsive** styling for all devices.

### **Key Features**

- **Expense Categorization**: Automatically calculates who owes what, _saving time_.
- **Group Management**: Easily add members and track expenses _by group_.
- **Intuitive Interface**: _User-friendly_ design for _seamless_ operation.
- **Conflict Reduction**: Eliminates manual calculation hassles, keeping _harmony_ in relationships.

**Perfect** for _travel groups_, **roommates**, or _event organizers_. This app brings **convenience**, ensuring social moments remain _joyful_ without _financial disputes_.
