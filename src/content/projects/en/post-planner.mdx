A comprehensive social media management platform that simplifies content creation and automated posting on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. This full-stack application empowers creators and businesses to manage their online presence with smart scheduling and AI-driven content.

Built on Next.js, it integrates NextAuth.js with Facebook authentication for seamless connectivity. The platform leverages the Meta Developers API for direct posting to Facebook and Instagram.

AI powers engaging content scripts and storylines, paired with ElevenLabs for realistic voice synthesis and HeyGen for personalized AI avatars, enabling high-quality multimedia content with minimal effort.

Key Features

- Smart scheduling for posting at optimal times.

- AI-driven content and script generation.

- High-quality voice synthesis and unique AI avatar creation.

Powered by TanStack Query for efficient data management and Supabase with PostgreSQL for robust database operations, this platform transforms creative ideas into impactful social media realities.
